    • About Point

    Point is a platform leveraging blockchain technology for merchants to run their loyalty reward programs on Ethereum network, bringing in transparency, interoperability, and convenience to both consumers and businesses.

    Why Point?

    Our platform helps businesses to issue their reward points as ERC20 tokens on the immutable Ethereum ledger, and allows customers to easily hold all their rewards in one location with our app.

    Utility Token

    We use a standardized utility token on Ethereum network known as ERC-20 token to represent gift card balance/rewards.

    Easy to use

    We provide consumers with a modern mobile application to manage all their rewards, and provide businesses with powerful SAAS solution to manage their rewards programs.

    Smart Contract

    Smart contract allows businesses to issue and define how they want the reward points to be used, giving businesses the ability to design powerful incentives.

    No learning curve

    Users do not need to remember their private keys. We derive users' key-pair deterministically based on their passphrase(email address) and salt(password).
